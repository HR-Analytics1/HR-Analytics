# Benq HR Analytics: Competition and Collaboration

---

## Overview  
This project provides a complete HR Analytics dashboard built using **Microsoft Power BI**,  
aiming to help management make data-driven decisions regarding employees' performance, satisfaction, attrition, and salary structure.  

The dashboard consists of multiple interactive pages that visualize key HR metrics and insights,  
allowing HR teams to identify trends, patterns, and issues across the organization.  

---

## Problem  
Organizations often face challenges like:  
- Difficulty in **tracking employee satisfaction and engagement**.  
- Lack of visibility into **why employees leave** and **what factors affect retention**.  
- Unclear understanding of **salary distribution and fairness**.  
- Manual reporting that consumes time and limits insight depth.  

---

## Solution  
This project solves these challenges by building a unified **Power BI HR Dashboard** that:  
- Integrates multiple datasets (Employee, Performance, Salary, Attrition).  
- Uses DAX calculations to create dynamic insights.  
- Offers an interactive and visual way to explore HR data.  
- Helps management improve employee satisfaction, retention, and payroll fairness.  

---

## Dataset Overview  
**Source:** Simulated HR dataset cleaned and modeled in Power BI  

**Main Columns Include:**  
- `EmployeeID`, `Gender`, `Age`, `Age Band`, `Department`, `JobRole`  
- `Education`, `Education Level`, `Marital Status`  
- `YearsAtCompany`, `PerformanceRating`, `SatisfactionLevel`, `Attrition`  
- `Salary`, `MonthlyIncome`, `OverTime`, `TrainingOpportunitiesTaken`  

**Tools & Techniques:**  
- Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)  
- Data Cleaning in Power Query  
- KPI Cards, Bar/Column Charts, Donut Charts, Line Charts, Scatter Plots  

---

## Pages Overview  

### Home Page  
**Purpose:**  
Acts as a **navigation hub** to access the main report pages.  

**Includes:**  
- Buttons/links to each analytical page: 
- Employee Satisfaction & Rating Overview    
- Attrition Dashboard   
- Salary Dashboard   
- Tittle and simple layout for easy access.

**Benefit:**  
Provides a clean and intuitive start point for users to move between dashboards easily.  

---

### Employee Satisfaction & Rating Overview  
**Purpose:**  
Analyze how employees feel about their work environment and how that relates to performance.  

**Visuals Include:**  
- Satisfaction Index, Performance Index, Average Worklife balance, Engagement Gap, and Training Participation Rate (KPI Cards)
- Satisfaction Index By Field
- Performance Index by Work Life Balance
- Satisfaction Over Years 
- Training Opportunities Taken by Manager Rating
- Performance Index Over Years
- Training Taken% by Department

**Benefit:**  
Helps HR understand what drives motivation and productivity,  
and where improvements can increase employee engagement.  

---

### Attrition Dashboard  
**Purpose:**  
Investigate the reasons behind employee turnover and identify risk areas.  

**Visuals Include:**  
- Employee Count, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Average Age,v and Average Years (KPI Cards)  
- Attrition by YearsAtCompany
- Performance Index by Work Life Balance
- Attrition by Job
- Attrition by Age
- Attrition Count by Income Band

**Benefit:**  
Helps identify patterns behind employee exits and supports better retention strategies.  

---

### Salary Dashboard  
**Purpose:**  
Understand salary distribution and identify any imbalance in pay structures.  

**Visuals Include:**  
- Number Of Employees, Total Salary, Average Salary, Salary Range, Max Salary, and Min Salary (KPI Cards)  
- Average Salary by Department, Job Role, Gender, Age Group, and Education  
- Overtime Impact on Salary Average
- Top 5 Job Roles by Average Salary

**Benefit:**  
Provides transparency in payroll analysis, detects pay gaps,  
and helps design fair and competitive compensation strategies.
